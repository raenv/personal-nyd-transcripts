##### [a letter received by The Manhattan Company]

#### May 30th, 1894

My dear Mr. Thatcher,

I hope you’ll forgive me the exceedingly short interval that exists between this letter and my last. The situation with the theatre has become more urgent and I must request your reply to my earlier missive regarding a loan. Please reply with haste.

I appreciate any assistance you can provide and I am,

Yours sincerely,

J. S. Fogg
