##### [a telegram received by C.X. Chambers]

#### 1941 DECEMBER 7  PM  4 13

MOVE YOUR TRIP UP. COME TO LOS ANGELES IMMEDIATELY. WORLD MAY BE ENDING. 

WE WON’T BE ABLE TO STAY OUT OF IT NOW.
