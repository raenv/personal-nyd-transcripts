##### [a clipping from a London newspaper, kept in J.S. Fogg’s personal diary]

#### February 2nd, 1909

“Wonderment and Spectacle Abound! Renowned American Illusionist Sets His Sights on Europe”

Fogg the Fearless has long been known for his daring escapes and dazzling illusions. After several successful tours through his homeland of North America, Fogg and his wonderful show are making their way across the pond, first stopping in London and Edinburgh before continuing on to the Continent. 

Hailed by many as the only worthy rival to the beloved Harry Houdini, Fogg is known most for pioneering several escapology feats that not even Mr. Houdini himself has attempted. After first making a splash at the Great Columbian Exposition in Chicago, Illinois, Fogg the Fearless has continued to escalate his acts, stunning audiences and leaving amazement in his wake. 

Mark your calendars for the spring, for you will not want to miss the once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to witness this Great Magician in the flesh!
