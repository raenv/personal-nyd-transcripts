Hello and welcome to the (highly) unofficial New Year's Day content archive!

I don't exactly know how you would've found this website, but if you've ended up here and haven't subscribed to the Atypical Artists' Patreon, I'd like to ask you to leave. I mean, there's not really anything I can do to stop you, but seriously. Please.

Long story short, the way the letters are formatted on both Tumblr and the Patreon is extremely broken for me, to the point it's basically impossible to get anything coherent out of it. Major disclaimer: much of the content on this website is paywalled. I do not intend to leak this content with anyone, I just want to be able to access it myself since I really love this podcast.

I don't want to be a dick. I don't want to share anything that shouldn't be shared. I haven't advertised this website anywhere. So if you've somehow magically found it, please just do the right thing and leave. Thank you :]

This website hosts the transcripts of every released episode and every monthly missive, all organized into chronological order for better continuity. It also hosts the raw audio of the podcast episodes, available through an audio player on their pages, and sourced from the Atypical Artists' YouTube channel.

This site was modeled after (and forked from) snarp's Magnus Archives Transcripts Archive Archive A, as well as james-spooky's Red Valley Transcripts Archive, which I helped work on :].
